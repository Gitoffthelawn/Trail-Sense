Grouse
A medium-sized game bird with mottled feathers that provide excellent camouflage. Grouse are primarily ground-dwelling and feed on a variety of foods, including vegetation, seeds, berries, and insects. They are most active during dawn and dusk and can often be found foraging near forest edges or open clearings.
